INTRODUCTION

THIS IS A MUSIC PROJECT FOR AUDIOVISUAL PARTICIPANTS IN COLLABORATION WITH WELL-KNOWN VOCALOID MUSICIAN DECO*27 AND MUSICIAN TAKUYA YAMANAKA. THIS PROJECT ADOPTS A SPECIAL SYSTEM WHERE THE AUDIENCE JUDGES THROUGH VOTING, WHICH WILL ALSO IMPACTS THE PLOT TREND. THE AUDIENCE PLAYS THE ROLE OF A GUARD WHO RULED ON 10 "MURDERERS" IN A PRISION CALLED MILGRAM, AND MADE THEIR OWN JUDGMENTS AFTER LEARNING ABOUT THEIR EXPERIENCES AND STORIES BASED ON THE CHARACTERS' SONGS. THEIR SONG IS GENERATED FROM A SPECIFIC HIGH TECH MACHINE, IT READS THEIR MENTAL IMAGE. EVERY AUDIENCE GETS ONE VOTE A DAY FOR EACH TRIAL TO DETERMINE IF THEY'RE "GUILTY" OR "INNOCENT". WE CAN SEE THE RESULT FOR EACH TRIAL WHEN IT ENDS, AND THE FINAL RESULT COMES OUT AFTER ALL THREE TRIAL IS ENDED. PRISONERS WILL BE SENTENCED TO DEATH IF THEIR FINAL RESULT IS CONSIDERED AS "GUILTY".
